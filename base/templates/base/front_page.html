Tax process

Step 1. Request a Tax card

Estimate income, travel expenses and other deductions (YEL insurance, etc.) at MyTax

Step 2. Register YEL insurance when applicable

It is mandatory for you to take out YEL insurance if you are between 18-67 years old, self-employed for at least four consecutive months and your income is at least 8 575,45 per year. Some of companies sell YEL insurance are Elo, Ilmarinen, Varma and Veritas.

Step 3. Income Register

Report an income earner's data to the Incomes Register here.

Step 4. Pay tax

For every invoice, you pay your income tax at MyTax.

Step 5. Pay YEL insurance

For every invoice, you pay your percentage of income using the online service of your pension insurance company.

Step 6. Pre-completed tax return

In March or April, you will receive a pre-completed tax return. If there is no need for corrections or additions in your return, you do not have to do anything. If you want to make corrections, the deadline for them is in May. You can check the deadline date on your tax return and in MyTax.
